What is Tonika?

Tonika is a routing and security infrastructure for independent distributed social networks. Users of Tonika are equal citizens in a private and secure Internet of their own. Every user obtains a permanent anonymous URL, under which they can publish content or services. Low-level physical message exchange occurs only between pairs of related users in an encrypted and authenticated fashion. Novel routing algorithms allow for establishing bilaterally anonymous and encrypted communication between any pair of users (specified by their URLs), in a manner that scales.

Open source.
